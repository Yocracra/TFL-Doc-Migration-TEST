## 1\. Purpose of This Safety Manual

This document outlines the minimum safety information required to operate the Form Labs 3 Resin Printer in the Fab Lab. It focuses on:

  * Hazards that can cause injury or damage
  * Required protective measures
  * Emergency response procedures



This document does not teach you how to operate the machine. Refer to the linked Operations Manual for usage instructions.

## 2\. Who May Use This Machine

This machine may be used by:

  * The Fab Lab users.



Usage conditions:

  * Staff present
  * Operations Manual Acknowledgement
  * Safety Manual Acknowledgement
  * Cleaning Tools Prepared
  * No loose fitted clothing or jewelry while using the machine



## 3\. Primary Hazards

Be aware of the following hazards when using this machine:

  * Chemical 


  * Resin used in SLA can irritate sensitive areas, such as the eyes and skin, and should not be consumed.


  * Regarding skin and eyes, according to [Formlabs](<https://www.google.com/url?q=https://support.formlabs.com/s/article/Resin-Care?language%3Den_US&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1776804197000129&usg=AOvVaw3sKWRJxwEyWMcAnEcQ3abI>), “Eye contact may cause eye irritation. Skin contact may cause an allergic skin reaction.”


  * Isopropyl alcohol - contact or consumption may cause irritation and damage. Minimize exposure without protection.


  * Mechanical 


  * Moving parts on the printer, optic laser, and bed platform during printer run.



## 4\. Required Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

The following PPE is suggested when operating this machine:

  * Nitrile Gloves (required)



## 5\. General Safety Rules

Always follow these rules when using this machine:

  * Never bypass safety interlocks or guards
  * Never allow contact of resin in its uncured state with your skin, eyes or other 


  * Regarding skin and eyes, according to [Formlabs](<https://www.google.com/url?q=https://support.formlabs.com/s/article/Resin-Care?language%3Den_US&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1776804197001458&usg=AOvVaw13cQYq6fs2JDJL7mz81EoF>), “Eye contact may cause eye irritation. Skin contact may cause an allergic skin reaction.”


  * Keep hands, hair, clothing, and loose items clear of moving parts
  * Do not operate the machine if you are unsure how to proceed



## 6\. Emergency Stop & Safety Features

Know the location and function of the following before operating the machine:

  * Emergency Stop: For emergency stop, perform a power disconnect (no emergency stop button)
  * Power Disconnect: Back of the machine to the left 
  * Safety Interlocks / Covers: Never operate or try with the Orange UV protective cover open



For more information, refer to the: [FormLabs 3 Resin Printer Operations Manual](<FormLabs 3 Resin Printer Operations Manual.md>)

## 7\. Unsafe Conditions – Stop Immediately If:

Stop using the machine and notify Fab Lab staff immediately if any of the following occur:

  * The optic platform, build plate, or resin tank starts to shake and gets stuck
  * Abnormal sounds start to appear or vibrations move the machine
  * Resin starts leaking and spilling
  * Any error appears on the touchscreen



Do not attempt to repair major issues yourself. 

## 8\. Incident & Injury Response

If an incident occurs:

  1. Stop the machine using the emergency stop if safe to do so
  2. Move to a safe distance
  3. Notify Fab Lab staff immediately
  4. For injuries, staff will follow Fab Lab [emergency procedures](<../../../Fab Lab Operations/Fab Lab Safety & Emergency Manual.md>)



## 9\. Chemical & Material Safety 

The Formlabs 3 printer system uses  photopolymer resin and isopropyl alcohol:

Care:

  1. Use Nitrile Gloves  whenever possible contact or resin may occur, or during handling alcohol


  1. Beware of where and what you touch when using the gloves, and if any trace of either alcohol or resin is left, clean with a paper towel. (For information on cleaning, refer to the [cleaning manual](<../Wasing and Curing Machines/Formlabs Cleaning Manual.md>))


  2. Always keep the Orange UV case on unless something needs to be done inside the machine.



Disposal:

  1. Never dispose of any resin or isopropyl alcohol used on the drain



On contact:

  1. If any resin touches the skin, wash the area with soap or water. Never use any product with alcohol as a hand sanitizer.


  1. As said by the manufacturer,[ ](<https://www.google.com/url?q=https://support.formlabs.com/s/article/Resin-Care?language%3Den_US&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1776804197005377&usg=AOvVaw237yZVvM14ke3-BRpBslBT>)“To remove resin from skin, wash thoroughly with soap and water. Do not wash skin with products containing alcohol, like hand sanitizer, or any other solvents.”
