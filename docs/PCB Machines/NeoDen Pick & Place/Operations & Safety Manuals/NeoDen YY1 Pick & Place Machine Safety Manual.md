## 1\. Purpose of This Safety Manual

This document outlines the minimum safety information required to operate the NeoDen YY1 pick & place machine in The Fab Lab. It focuses on:

  * Hazards that can cause injury or damage
  * Required protective measures
  * Emergency response procedures



This document does not teach you how to operate the machine. Refer to the linked Operations Manual for usage instructions.

## 2\. Who May Use This Machine

This machine may be used by:

  * All Fab Lab Users



Usage conditions:

  * Staff Presence
  * User must have acknowledged this safety manual
  * User must have completed required machine orientation
  * Machine must be scheduled through approved booking system



## 3\. Primary Hazards

Be aware of the following hazards when using this machine:

  * Electrical Hazard: Risk of electric shock from internal power supply, exposed wiring during maintenance, or damaged power cables.
  * Mechanical Hazard: Rapid movement of the placement head (X/Y axis gantry),  z-axis nozzle motion, feeder loading mechanisms, conveyor movement (if installed), and keeping hands clear during operation.



## 4\. Required Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

The following PPE is required when operating this machine:

  * No special PPE is required beyond standard Fab Lab attire.



## 5\. General Safety Rules

Always follow these rules when using this machine:

  * Never bypass safety covers or protective panels
  * Never operate with the housing removed
  * Keep hands clear of the placement head during motion
  * Never reach into the machine while it is running
  * Do not leave the machine unattended during active placement cycles
  * Ensure feeders are secured before starting a job
  * Confirm emergency stop location before powering on
  * Do not attempt hardware repairs unless authorized
  * If unsure at any time, STOP and ask staff.



## 6\. Emergency Stop & Safety Features

Know the location and function of the following before operating the machine:

  * Main Control Interface: The integrated 7-inch capacitive touchscreen located on the front-right of the machine.
  * Emergency Stop Location: The large red mushroom button located on the top-right or front-right corner of the chassis.
  * Key User Interaction Points:


  * Nozzle Changer: The automatic tool changer area at the rear of the work table.
  * Feeders: The left and right side-mounted tape feeder banks.
  * Vision System: The upward-looking camera (for component alignment) and downward-looking camera (for PCB fiducials).



## 7\. Unsafe Conditions – Stop Immediately If:

Stop using the machine and notify Fab Lab staff immediately if any of the following occur:

  * Unexpected noise, vibration, or erratic motion occurs
  * Placement head moves outside programmed bounds
  * Smoke, sparks, or burning odors appear
  * Feeder jams or mechanical binding occurs
  * Software crashes or communication is lost
  * Power cable is damaged



Do not attempt to diagnose or repair major issues yourself.

## 8\. Incident & Injury Response

If an incident occurs:

  1. Stop the machine using the emergency stop if safe to do so
  2. Move to a safe distance
  3. Notify Prototyping Studio staff immediately
  4. For injuries, staff will follow Prototyping Studio emergency procedures



## 9\. Acknowledgement

By using this machine, you acknowledge that:

  * You have reviewed this Safety Manual
  * You understand the primary hazards and required precautions
  * You will follow all Fab Lab safety rules
  * You will ask staff for help if you are unsure or uncomfortable
